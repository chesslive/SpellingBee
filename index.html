📘 Tutorial

Welcome! Here's how to play:

  • Click "Hear Word" to hear a word.
  • Spell it correctly to earn points and XP.
  • Complete 5 words daily to keep your streak alive!
  • Reach higher levels to unlock Challenge, Ranked, Live Play, and Power-ups (level 35).
  • Customize your profile with avatars, colors, borders, and titles!
  • Claim daily bonus for extra points.
  • After 3 incorrect attempts, the word is revealed.
  • Click "Show Meaning" to see the definition without seeing the word.
  • You gain points every time you level up (more points for higher levels).
  • Net worth now includes all points you've ever earned (spent + current).
  • Public Mode: Submit your own words (they are reviewed) or play words submitted by others.
  • Unseen Mode: Coming soon!
